Rock Valley College

Welcome To Rock Valley College

At first Glance Rock Valley College looks like any other school in the United States but then you take your first class. That first class you will meet so many different people with different backgrounds and experiences, learning at Rock Valley does not just happen from teacher to student it happens from student to student interactions. Students sharing past life experiences and trying to help each other grow as humans and to become part of the community together as a school. Welcome to Rock Valley College it makes a great home to learn more about yourself and to get a degree or certificate to better yoursefl for the future.

When you first start at Rock Valley you may have an idea of what you want out of college be that experiences or a certain degree. Rock Valley will do an amazing thing that all schools have and that is professors but Rock Valley takes the gold medal in that department, with professors that have experience in the job field that they now teach about. That will share stories to help students learn and to help them decide if that field is right for them they will guide students, who wish to follow that path or help you pick something else.

While you take classes here there are tons of clubs and groups to join and they have an event where most clubs will be on the bridge leading to the student center, waiting to give you even more experiences to help prepare you for future. Wether the club be focused on fun or the help further your learning they all have a great sense of comradery.

My experience in Woodward Technology Center

I am in the cisco networking porgram at Rock Valley so most of my clsses are at the Woodward Technology Center on main campus. I came to Rock Valley as a high school graduate who was not quite sure what they wanted to do yet and I took some basic intro classes. At Rock Valley everyone takes an intro to computers class which sparked my intrest in making a career out of computers. That class set me up to spend a lot of hours in this building. The images above are of the lobby and one of the many study areas on campus this area has been home to long hours by me.

Woodward is a great place on campus where I have met some cool proffessors and some even greater students. I have spent time after class talking many nights learning more on a differnt way to code or getting a second opinion on a life decision. The Woodward Technology Center has shown me more in 1 building than other whole school, I have visited, proffesors that actually care about what happens to you after college in a career.

Rock Valley College is a great school where everyone who comes here grows in some way. Some will grow closer to others some will meet their life partner and others will move on to make another school a better place from what they learned at Rock Valley College.

My recommendation about Rock Valley

When you go to make your schedule be sure to talk to a counsellor. Reason for seeing them is they can guide you in what to take and when to take it. I recommend taking STU—100 as soon as you can fit it in your schedule. Reason being that it will help you find what resources are available to you through out your time at Rock Valley College.
